About Me

I'm Badreddine El Habra, a Computer Science and AI student at Al Akhawayn University in Morocco, passionate about building technology that solves real problems. My journey started early — running my family's store at 13 taught me that understanding people's needs is just as important as the tools you use to serve them. That blend of entrepreneurial grounding and technical curiosity has shaped everything I do, from developing AI-powered applications to designing digital experiences for local businesses.

I specialize in full-stack web development and applied AI, with hands-on experience in React, Node.js, Python, and computer vision. I've built and shipped products in competitive startup environments, including an AI fitness coaching app that used real-time pose estimation to analyze movement — and I've taken ideas from Lean Canvas to live prototype. Whether I'm crafting a clean, responsive website for a local café or architecting a data-driven application, I bring the same attention to detail and commitment to quality.

Beyond the code, I'm driven by a deep belief that technology should be accessible and impactful — especially in emerging markets. I'm currently exploring how AI can transform education for French-system students across Morocco and Francophone Africa, while also helping local businesses in Larache establish a meaningful presence online. I don't just build things; I build things that matter, for people who need them most.
